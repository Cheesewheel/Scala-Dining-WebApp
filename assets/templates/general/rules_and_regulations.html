The use of this site implements the following rules and restrictions:

Claiming and using dining slots:

  • Each day 3 dining slots can be claimed
  • Dining slots can be claimed till 18:00 if slots are still availlable
  • Each association can only claim one slot per day
  • The kitchen can be used between 16:30 and 19:30
  • The default sign-up deadline is 15:30, but can be altered by the dining list owner

  • A dininglist can be removed as long as its minimum users is not yet reached
  • The minimum users can be at most 6
  • People can not join a dining list if the maximum number of diners is reached
  • Dining list owner can add/remove people at all times and ignore the rule above
  • The maximum needs to be at least 12 people

  • Dining list owner is required to fill in the shop/cook/and clean markers on dining lists
